Protržená přehrada ⭐ Best pick

Desná → dam ruins along Bílá Desná river
Distance
4–6 km
Difficulty
Easy–moderate
Stroller
❌ No
☀️ Best in sunshine 🌧️ Muddy when wet 🎒 Carrier needed
Forest trail along the Bílá Desná river to the historical dam ruins. Educational panels, wooden bridges, water features. Short segments perfect for stopping and exploring.
With kids: Best ratio of adventure to effort. 5yo walks most of it, 2yo in carrier for steeper parts. Built-in "wow" moments keep them going.
📍 Open in Mapy.cz

Jizerka

Peat bogs, boardwalks & Safírový potok
Distance
3–5 km
Difficulty
Easy
Stroller
⚠️ Partial
☀️ Best on calm day 💨 Exposed, no shelter ❄️ No cover from cold 👶 Boardwalks OK, trails no
Open peat bog landscape with wooden boardwalks. Safírový potok (Sapphire Creek) — rocks and water for kids to play with. Wide-open views of the Jizera Mountains.
With kids: Zero-stress option. No real elevation. Bring extra layers — more exposed than it looks from the parking.
📍 Open in Mapy.cz

Rozhledna Štěpánka

Forest climb with a tower reward at the top
Distance
4–6 km
Difficulty
Moderate
Stroller
❌ No
☀️ Clear sky for views 🌧️ Slippery climb 💨 Exposed tower top 🎒 Carrier needed
Forest paths with a short steep section leading to a stone lookout tower. Panoramic views of Jizerské hory and Krkonoše on clear days.
With kids: Built-in motivation — "we're climbing the tower!" Expect to carry the 2yo for the uphill. The tower itself is the payoff.
📍 Open in Mapy.cz
4

Přehrada Souš

Flat paved road around a reservoir — stroller paradise
Distance
2–4 km
Difficulty
Very easy
Stroller
✅ Yes
🌧️ Paved, works in rain ❄️ Works in cold 💨 Open water, windy 👶 Fully stroller-friendly
Wide asphalt road around a reservoir. Used by cyclists and inline skaters — surface is smooth and flat throughout. Flexible length — just turn around when you want.
With kids: The stroller-safe fallback. Bring a balance bike for the 5yo. More of a calm walk than an adventure — perfect for tired days or bad weather.
📍 Open in Mapy.cz

Quick Tips

📱

Offline mapy

Download Mapy.cz offline maps. Signal is unreliable in the mountains — don't count on it.

🥪

Svačina je must

Pack food and water. There's nothing on the trails — no kiosks, no restaurants mid-route.

🎒

Krosna > kočár

Child carrier beats stroller on 80% of trails. Only Souš is truly stroller-safe.

🔄

Otočit se včas

Turn back early = win. Don't push to finish at all costs — especially with little ones.
